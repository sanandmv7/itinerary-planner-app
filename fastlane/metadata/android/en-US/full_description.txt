Itinerary Planner is your go-to companion for organizing and managing your travel plans. Whether you're planning a quick weekend getaway or an extended international adventure, this app helps you keep all your trip details in one place.

Features:

* Create and manage multiple trip plans
* Add itinerary items including dates, times, and costs
* Visualize your trip timeline
* Track your travel budget
* Attach images to your trips for easy memory keeping
* User-friendly interface for effortless planning

This open-source app respects your privacy and doesn't require an internet connection. All your trip data is stored locally on your device.

Perfect for:

* Frequent travelers
* Vacation planners
* Anyone who loves to keep their trips well-organized

Start planning your next adventure today with Itinerary Planner!
